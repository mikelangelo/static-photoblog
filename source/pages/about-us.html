About Mikelangelo Photography

Back in the 1900s—yes, before smartphones took over—I started photography as a way to collect reference images for my art studies. Armed with film and a dark room, I discovered a love for photography that just wouldn’t let me go.

Now, I’ve channeled that obsession into capturing everything from roaring motorsports and elegant dressage to still life and portraiture.

I can be reached on via Facebook or mike@mikelangelo.com
